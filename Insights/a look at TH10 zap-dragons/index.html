a look at TH10 zap-dragons

by Progamer published on: 10/03/2022 updated on: 20/01/2025

At Townhall 10 zap dragons is one of the most effective
and most used strategies, because of it's ease of use,
however it is still effective in townhalls above and below.

zap dragons uses lightning-spells to take out air-defenses
to easilly clear out a base and get 100%.

zap dragons is so strong in fact that the air defense of
TH10 was given an extra 10hp just to take one extra zap
of the level you can unlock at TH10.

However, it should be noted that this doesnt apply to lower
Town Halls, allowing you to have an easier time attacking TH9s
due to being able to take out all 4 Air Defenses with spells

To use the strategy you just need to spam lightning on
the airdefenses and spam your dragons and some loons to the side of the
one that's left, which should be the easiest to reach.

If you can take out other buildings while spamming the Lightnings
you should prioritze air sweepers and single target inferno-towers.

I hope this guide helps you a bit with your own TH10 attacks.
